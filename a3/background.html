Project Background

Motivation

The tourism industry is very valuable to the state of Victoria, prior to the pandemic forecasts redirected that the tourism industry would contribute to 56% of the economic growth for the next decade in visitor expenditure ("Value of Victoria's tourism industry", 2020).

Our goal was to help remedy this economic downturn with a web platform that would connect tourists with local Melbourne businesses. Since technology is serving to keep us functional during times of quarantine, it can also help to connect us post-pandemic. We envision MelLocal as an application that can help to kickstart the visitor economy during this downturn.

Not only that, the main reason for us to take over this assignment is to target international students and help them out in a most efficient way. According to a survey, over 200,000 students from 170 countries choose Melbourne for education purposes annually. The majority of them come to Melbourne with high expectations, wishing to experience the best of Melbourne. Due to the high costs of existing tourist websites, who often promote middle men, most of the students are limited by cost in experiencing what Melbourne has to offer. Combined with a high study load, international students would like an efficient and straight to the point way of finding Melbourne experiences within their price range.

By building MelLocal, we aim to provide the best solutions to Melbourne visitors with proper guidance and a reasonable cost. We believe that most of the students/tourists as well as Melbourne businesses can benefit from a win/win in terms of connecting to each other and creating value via our platform.

Aim

We wanted to create a user-friendly tourist web application targeting international students as well as tourists who visit Melbourne. The aim of this website is to help out the customers to experience Melbourne with a reasonable cost. Our app named ‘MelLocal’ is a map which serves a purpose of directing Tourists to the best restaurants, bars and fun activities to do in Melbourne that are described as ‘experiences’ within our website pplication. It lists information for an experience such as an average cost price, customer reviews and ratings. It also allows the user to filter through the search results, such as price preferences and whether they want bars, restaurants or other experiences to appear in the search results. It allows the user to create their own profile to save favorite experiences to a designated special folder. Local businesses can sign-up to the app to promote their business and list services they offer by filling in details of their business.

Goals

Experience Review

This goal is about allowing the customers to place their feedback on the particular product or service they experienced through our website. This is one of the most effective feature which helps the owner to improve the website according to the needs of the customers. Most of the users would like to get a brief idea of the service before they make use of it. The feedback given by the past customers on that specific service will boost the confidence level in the user to experience the service provided.

User Registration

This goal is one of the most common and important functionality used in most of the websites. This particular goal is about allowing users to create accounts or/and login to their accounts to access the whole website and manage their visit of purpose according to their own preference. Customers only need to register themselves once, afterwards they can simply login to the website.The server stores the user details in the database and retrieves whenever the user logs in. Customers will be updated with all the promotions and deals by the contact information provided by the customer once they register with us. The user will have the ability to change the registration information in the settings if they want to.

Search Filtering

This goal is about allowing the end users to search products by the name, brand or category. Multiple filters may be applied to take a broad range of products and refine them into a more narrow selection,by allowing the end user to retrieve the most relevant search results based on the criteria they’ve selected. Our main objective of this feature is to help the customer achieve maximum product discoverability, reducing navigation friction and any potential disruptions along the customer path of purchase.

Business Advertisement

This specific goal is about allowing business users to advertise and gain more business from tourists and travellers. In order for this information to be displayed on the MelLocal website it needs to be uploaded by the business owner by simply registering with us.This information includes data such as location, average spend of customers, website, phone number etc.. Business users will also need to provide a category in which their business will be displayed in. After a successful completion, the business ads will be displayed on the main platform in front of a high volume of people even if they are not searching.

Display Business Information

This is the most important feature displayed based on Business advertisement to target the right set of customers. Majority of the business advertised in MelLocal will be new to the business industry. Our main goal is to provide a brief explanation about the business which will help the fellow customers to get a good idea about that particular business and to increase the understanding level of each business advertised. This will drive the customer to make the right choice in a most efficient way.

Scope

The scope of the project is to build a MVC (model/view/controller) application with objects to represent:

  • Users
  • Business Users
  • Business in Melbourne
  • Reviews
  • Categories

MelLocal’s goal is to allow users to search those businesses by category and view their cost information (average spend by customers as provided by businesses). Customers should also be able to perform a filtered search and leave reviews for businesses based in Melbourne, a review is an object that references both a user (who left the review) and a business (that they are leaving the review for). Users associated with a business should be able to see their business’ reviews, similarly regular users should be able to view past reviews that they have left for businesses they have visited. The purpose of a User account on MelLocal is for saving places (referred to as favourites) in the application.
