Project Background

Motivation

The tourism industry is very valuable to the state of Victoria, prior to the pandemic forecasts redirected that the tourism industry would contribute to 56% of the economic growth for the next decade in visitor expenditure ("Value of Victoria's tourism industry", 2020).

Our goal was to help remedy this economic downturn with a web platform that would connect tourists with local Melbourne businesses. Since technology is serving to keep us functional during times of quarantine, it can also help to connect us post-pandemic. We envision MelLocal as an application that can help to kickstart the visitor economy during this downturn.

Not only that, the main reason for us to take over this assignment is to target international students and help them out in a most efficient way. According to a survey, over 200,000 students from 170 countries choose Melbourne for education purposes annually. The majority of them come to Melbourne with high expectations, wishing to experience the best of Melbourne. Due to the high costs of existing tourist websites, who often promote middle men, most of the students are limited by cost in experiencing what Melbourne has to offer. Combined with a high study load, international students would like an efficient and straight to the point way of finding Melbourne experiences within their price range.

By building MelLocal, we aim to provide the best solutions to Melbourne visitors with proper guidance and a reasonable cost. We believe that most of the students/tourists as well as Melbourne businesses can benefit from a win/win in terms of connecting to each other and creating value via our platform.

Aims & Goals

Scope

The scope of the project is to build a MVC (model/view/controller) application with objects to represent:

  • Users
  • Business Users
  • Business in Melbourne
  • Reviews
  • Categories

MelLocal’s goal is to allow users to search those businesses by category and view their cost information (average spend by customers as provided by businesses). Customers should also be able to perform a filtered search and leave reviews for businesses based in Melbourne, a review is an object that references both a user (who left the review) and a business (that they are leaving the review for). Users associated with a business should be able to see their business’ reviews, similarly regular users should be able to view past reviews that they have left for businesses they have visited. The purpose of a User account on MelLocal is for saving places (referred to as favourites) in the application.
